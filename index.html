About

The Story Behind
The Lens

I'm Luke, a new photographer finding my voice through the lens. What started as a curiosity quickly became an obsession: chasing golden hour light, getting lost in city streets at dusk, and learning to see the extraordinary in everyday moments.

I'm just getting started, but every shoot teaches me something new. I'm drawn to raw, honest frames, the kind that make you feel something before you even know why. This is the beginning of my visual journey, and I'm here to grow with every shot.
